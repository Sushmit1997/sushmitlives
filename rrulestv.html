RRulesTV

Let's go back in time. It's May of 2012 when my love for filmmaking really started. Back then my father had a Sony handycam and a Macbook with iMovie software installed. My tenth standard 'SLC' exam had recently been over and I was free to do whatever I wanted to do. I was experimenting with the software and thought, "Why not make a movie?". That was when the fire started burning inside me and my love for filmmaking hasn't gone yet. I may not be so active nowadays in this field but deep inside I'm still the same 2012 guy who loves shooting movies and editing them. 'RRulesTV' was the name I thought for my channel. Please enjoy some of my short movies. By any means, these are not yours today's movie standard but they're mine and I love them.
